    Inspiration

    Why pharmacy?

    I want to be a part of saving people's lives. Because preserving life is what I think is the most valuable thing in doing good. And another thing is that in our country there are very few medical personnel. Therefore, I want to be another person who becomes a medical personnel in order to help relieve the burden of many medical personnel. And this career is a stable career that allows me to lighten my family's burden.
